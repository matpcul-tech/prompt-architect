Common questions

What AI models does this work with?+
Prompt Architect builds structured prompts for any AI — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Llama, or any other model. The AI Enhancement feature uses Claude Sonnet under the hood.
Do I need prompt engineering experience?+
Not at all. The frameworks guide you field-by-field with smart placeholders and real-time scoring. The Playbook teaches fundamentals as you go. Most users see improvement on their first prompt.
Is my data private?+
Yes. Your prompts are stored securely and never used to train AI models. AI Enhancement calls are processed with standard privacy protections and immediately discarded.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel in one click. Your saved prompts remain accessible on the free plan.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?+
Free gives you all 5 frameworks, scoring, templates, and 10 saves. Pro adds AI Enhancement, unlimited saves, custom templates, and usage analytics. Free is genuinely useful — Pro makes you faster.
